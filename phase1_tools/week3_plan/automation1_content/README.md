# Automation 1 — Content Repurposing

## Before (manual process)
- I manually rewrite one long article or workshop note into several short social posts, email snippets, and slide bullets by hand.

## After (automated)
- What the automation does: source text -> Groq structured JSON -> content-queue row, in ~15 seconds
- Tested on 3 real pieces of content: all produced usable captions

## Time saved
- Manual time per piece (3 platforms): ~25 min
- Automated time per piece: ~15 sec
- Weekly time saved (at my current volume): ~100 min/week (~1.7 hrs), at 4 pieces/week

## Hypothesis
- I will automate the repetitive step of turning one piece of content into multiple formats so I can publish faster without retyping everything.
